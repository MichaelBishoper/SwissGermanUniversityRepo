FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What background is recommended for Mechatronics?

A strong interest in mathematics, physics, and how things work is ideal. Familiarity with basic programming is helpful but not required; the program starts from fundamentals.

Is there a mandatory internship?

Yes. Students complete internships in relevant companies, allowing them to apply mechatronics concepts in a professional setting and build early industry connections.
