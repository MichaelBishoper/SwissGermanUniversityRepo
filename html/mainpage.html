What is Double Degree and Why is it useful?

Read More:

A Double Degree is an academic program allowing students to earn two distinct degrees, often from different universities (domestic & international) or fields, in a shorter time than pursuing them separately, offering broader skills, enhanced global experience, and better job competitiveness by combining coursework into a single program.
