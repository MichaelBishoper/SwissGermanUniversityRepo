Pre-Requisites for Registration (Important)

Dokumen Yang Dibutuhkan

1. 1 salinan raport kelas 10, 11, dan 12 yang dilegalisir oleh Kepala Sekolah.
2. 1 fotokopi KTP / SIM / Paspor untuk pelajar lokal.
3. 1 lembar Paspor & KITAS untuk Sekolah Internasional.
4. 2 buah pas foto berukuran 4 X 6 cm (berwarna, dengan latar belakang merah).
5. 1 fotokopi Kartu Keluarga.
6. 1 fotokopi Surat Keterangan Keluarga (Kartu Keluarga).
7. Surat keterangan dokter yang menyatakan bahwa siswa tidak menderita buta warna (khusus untuk siswa Life Sciences).
8. 1 lembar fotokopi ijazah SMA / SMK / MA yang dilegalisir oleh kepala sekolah (SETELAH DITERBITKAN).
9. 1 lembar fotokopi Transkrip Nilai (Surat Keterangan Hasil Ujian Nasional SMA / SMK / MA) yang dilegalisir oleh kepala sekolah (SETELAH DITERBITKAN).
10. Calon lulusan luar negeri atau sekolah menengah internasional harus melegalisasi (Penyetaraan) ijazah sekolah menengah melalui Kementerian Pendidikan Nasional Republik Indonesia atau mengikuti IJAZAH Paket C.


Prosedur Pendaftaran

1. Pendaftaran
Pendaftaran penerimaan tahun 2025/2026 akan mulai pada tanggal 31 Juli 2026. Anda bisa daftar di sini dan pastikan Anda mengisi formulir dengan lengkap dan benar.
2. Biaya Pendaftaran
The payment link will be sent after the candidate successfully completes the registration process. The registration link will be sent via WhatsApp and email provided during registration.
3. Test
Tes Masuk terdiri dari tes tertulis Bahasa Inggris dan Matematika. Tautan Tes Masuk akan dikirim ke email Anda. silakan ikuti tes maksimal dua hari setelah Anda menerima email.
4. Interview
Wawancara dapat dilakukan melalui platform online atau tatap muka di Kampus SGU The Prominence Tower Alam Sutera, sesuai jadwal yang Anda isi di formulir pendaftaran online.

Required Documents

1. 1 copy of the report cards for grades 10, 11, and 12 legalized by the School Principal.
2. 1 photocopy of ID Card / Driver’s License / Passport for local students.
3. 1 sheet of Passport & KITAS for International School students.
4. 2 pieces of 4 X 6 cm passport photos (colored, with a red background).
5. 1 photocopy of the Family Card.
6. 1 photocopy of the Family Certificate (Family Card).
7. A medical certificate stating that the student is not color blind (specifically for Life Sciences students).
8. 1 photocopy of the high school diploma (SMA / SMK / MA) legalized by the school principal (AFTER ISSUED).
9. 1 photocopy of the Transcript (National Examination Result Letter for SMA / SMK / MA) legalized by the school principal (AFTER ISSUED).
10. Prospective graduates from overseas or international secondary schools must legalize (Equivalency) their secondary school diploma through the Ministry of National Education of the Republic of Indonesia or take the Paket C equivalency exam.


Registration Procedure

1. Registration
Registration for the 2025/2026 academic year will begin on July 31, 2026. You can register here and make sure you fill out the form completely and accurately.
2. Registration Fee
The payment link will be sent after the candidate successfully completes the registration process. The registration link will be sent via WhatsApp and the email provided during registration.
3. Test
The Entrance Test consists of a written English and Mathematics test. The Entrance Test link will be sent to your email. Please complete the test no later than two days after receiving the email.
4. Interview
The interview can be conducted online or in person at the SGU Campus, The Prominence Tower, Alam Sutera, according to the schedule you filled out in the online registration form.
