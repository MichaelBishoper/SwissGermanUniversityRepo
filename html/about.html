SGU at a Glance

Swiss German University (SGU) is Indonesia’s first international university with strong European connections, combining academic excellence, industry collaboration, and a modern campus in Alam Sutera.

Established in 2000 • International bachelor and master programs • Jointly initiated by Indonesia, Germany, Switzerland, and Austria

Dr. Dipl.-Ing. Samuel P. Kusumocahyo
Rector, Swiss German University
Welcome to Swiss German University.

From its humble beginning in 2000, SGU has grown into a pioneering international university in Indonesia, recognized for its strong European partnerships and practice-oriented learning culture. Our commitment is to develop graduates whose knowledge, experience, and character are ready for the demands of a global career.

At SGU, students learn in small classes, work closely with lecturers who have industry backgrounds, and gain real experience through internships and collaborative projects. We invite you to discover how SGU can be your gateway to global success.

International Approach & Modern Study Programs

SGU offers modern study programs with an international approach that combine theory, practice, and global exposure. Through cooperation with European universities and industry partners, students gain access to up-to-date technology, curriculum, and professional networks that prepare them for careers around the world.

Collaboration, Entrepreneurship, and Innovation

SGU encourages a culture of collaboration and entrepreneurship. Students work on projects that solve real-world challenges and are supported by lecturers who bring industry experience into the classroom. Our goal is to build graduates who are innovative, disciplined, and ready to contribute responsibly to society.

A Multicultural Learning Environment

Located in Alam Sutera, SGU brings together students and staff from diverse cultural and professional backgrounds. This creates a dynamic learning environment where ideas are exchanged freely and global perspectives are developed, helping students grow both academically and personally.

Our Campus in Alam Sutera

SGU is located at The Prominence Tower, Alam Sutera — a modern business and lifestyle district in Tangerang, Indonesia. The area provides easy access, supporting facilities, and a safe environment for students.

History of Swiss German University

2000
SGU was established as the first international university in Indonesia with an academic license recognized under Indonesian law, initiated through cooperation between Indonesia, Germany, Switzerland, and Austria.
2000–2010
SGU expanded its study programs in Engineering, Information Technology, Business, and Life Sciences, building strong partnerships with European universities and industry.
2010–2020
The university strengthened its internship programs, international collaborations, and research activities, producing graduates who are competitive both nationally and internationally.
Today
SGU continues to innovate with modern curricula, digital learning initiatives, and deeper collaboration with industry partners, while maintaining its focus on character, integrity, and social responsibility.
