Scholarship Opportunities

Swiss German University provides scholarship pathways for high-achieving students at both Bachelor and Master level. Explore the available schemes, required documents, and important deadlines below.

Bachelor Program

Apply for SGU Scholarship!

SGU invites SMA grade XII students to apply for academic and non-academic scholarships. Scholarships are awarded based on academic performance, achievements, and selection results.

This is a chance to access international-quality education with more affordable tuition fees. Review the requirements and submit your application through our online form.

Join This Program ⧉
The Documents Needed

Academic & Non-Academic Scholarships

  • Report card SMA grade X–XI with minimum average score of 80.
  • Recommendation letter from school teacher or principal.
  • Essay (min. 500 words) describing academic interests and goals.
  • Academic certificates (if any).
  • Participation in Academic Potential Test (APT) or equivalent entrance test.
  • Report card SMA grade X–XI.
  • Portfolio of achievements (sports, arts, organization, competition, etc.).
  • Recommendation letter from coach, mentor, or activity coordinator.
  • Certificates of achievements or awards.
  • Short motivation letter explaining contributions and future plans.

Important Deadlines

  1. Online Registration Period: dates according to the current intake.
  2. Announcement of Scholarship Results: communicated via email and official channels.

Please check the SGU website and your email frequently for updated schedules.

Master Program

Hello, prospective graduate students!

SGU offers exclusive scholarship opportunities for Master applicants, including SGU alumni and graduates from other universities who wish to advance their education.

Various schemes are available based on academic performance, professional experience, and selection results. Scholarships may include tuition fee discounts and other financial benefits.

Join This Program ⧉

Privilege (Scholarship by SGU)

Alumni Scholarships (Alumni SGU)

  • Applicable for SGU Bachelor alumni who pass the internal interview.
  • Minimum required GPA as specified in the current intake guideline.
  • Graduates with higher GPA may receive increasing tuition fee discounts for the Master program (e.g. 15%, 25%, 50% tiers).
  • Exact percentage and conditions follow the official SGU scholarship policy for the current academic year.

Academic Achievement Scholarships

  • Targeted at applicants with excellent academic performance at Bachelor level.
  • Based on cumulative GPA and supporting academic achievements or publications.
  • Requires submission of academic transcript and curriculum vitae (CV).
  • May include tuition fee reduction for selected semesters.

Alfa Silber Scholarship

  • Special cooperation scheme with SGU’s industrial partners.
  • Focus on applicants with strong professional potential in selected fields (engineering, IT, business, life sciences, etc.).
  • Additional interviews or technical tests may be required.
  • Detailed scheme and quota depend on agreement with the partner institution for each intake.

Document Requirement Procedure

  1. Academic transcript from the latest education level.
  2. Certificate of graduation (if already available).
  3. Motivational letter describing study and career goals.
  4. Copy of ID card / passport.

Privilege Industry Partnership

  1. Submit required documents (CV, academic transcript, certificates, portfolio, and motivation letter) to the designated SGU contact.
  2. Selected applicants may be invited to an online test and interview (English, academic competency, and motivation).
